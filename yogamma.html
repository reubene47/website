Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Yogamma

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. Yoga is typically practiced barefoot, but grip socks are fine if you prefer.

How do I book a class?

You can book through the contact form on this website, by calling/texting me at +44 7773 55628, or emailing yogamma@protonmail.com.

How do I get to Yogamma?

The exact address will be provided upon booking. Yogamma is located in my peaceful home studio in Fareham.

How much does it cost?

Your first class is free! After that, drop-in classes are £12 each or you can save with a 5-class pass for £50.

What should I bring?

Just bring yourself! Mats and props are provided, though you're welcome to bring your own mat if you prefer.
